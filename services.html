Services

Strategic Visioning, Planning and Design

We design for institutional change towards greater social equity, primarily in the cultural heritage space.

WE DESIGN EQUITY-BASED PROGRAMS AND PROJECTS.

  • We apply an equity lens to project design for cultural heritage organizations seeking to matter more to their communities.
  • Project development: We work with you to determine and shape your project’s concept, scope, focus and objectives. We can help with partner recruitment, project design, budgeting, evaluation (outcome and output identification) and other planning elements.
  • Project implementation: We work with you to launch and run your project or program. We help you implement new visions, and take your ideas to scale.
  • Narrative visioning: We help you set up equitable programs, projects, convenings, presentations, gallery or museum attractions and exhibits, etc., that tell more complete, non-patronizing, inclusive stories about a community or place.
  • Collections enrichment: We help you bring collections to life by engaging untapped or underrepresented communities to add their stories and perspectives. We guide you to present collections through the eyes and ears of the people at the heart of the stories that matter to your community, to make your collections more relevant and impactful.
  • Wayfinding: We can help you change your organization’s narrative, systems and existing structures to push toward your equity goals.
  • Organizational change: We can help you change your organization's internal culture to create a more equitable work environment for staff.
  • Equity audit: We help you understand where your organization stands in terms of equitable reflection of and engagement with your community. We help manage change toward social equity goals.

WE HELP YOU IMPROVE YOUR AUDIENCE OR USER EXPERIENCE.

  • We help you build more meaningful cultural heritage experiences by tapping relevant communities as co-designers.
  • We can help you improve your user or audience experience around your collections to engage new, diverse communities (or enrich community engagement).
  • Data collection: We conduct research to help you figure out how to be user driven, to let relevant communities help define your services, to better meet their needs.
  • Organizational assessment: We help you understand where your organization stands within your community. We help you better engage your community through a human centered design approach.
    • Institutional imagineering: We can help you reimagine an existing institution or program and build an archive, museum, library or program that truly reflects and serves the [full spectrum of] people in your community.

WE HELP YOU BETTER ENGAGE, SERVE, AND REFLECT YOUR COMMUNITY/ WE HELP YOU ENRICH YOUR OFFERINGS TO BETTER REFLECT YOUR COMMUNITY.

  • We help drive change in projects, processes, and people to give communities richer social return.
  • We help you lead in building equitable social systems by creating equitable and inclusive cultural heritage experiences.
  • Narrative amplification: We challenge dominant narratives. We help organizations build products, services, and programs that amplify the unheard and untold stories of marginalized people to increase equity in social, cultural and resource capacities.
  • Program enrichment: We apply innovation magic to drive change that benefits your organization and local communities. We help you innovate, make your offerings better, richer, less flat. We guide you to enrich experiences for your users and audiences.
  • Historical research: We conduct research to fill gaps created by historical inequities. We help marginalized and underrepresented groups find missing Information about their communities.

Field Building, Field Support

We work with cultural heritage practitioners to push the field forward. We guide people and organizations to be better leaders in the field by putting their communities first and practicing collaborative systems design.

WE CONVENE CULTURAL HERITAGE PRACTITIONERS TO SOLVE PROBLEMS IN THE FIELD.

  • We collaborate [with you and other partners] on workshops that challenge and expand thinking in various sectors of the cultural heritage field.
  • Experiential workshops: We convene cultural heritage practitioners to ask big questions relevant to the field and design solutions. We create events and gatherings that give people the space to do specific solutions-focused activities and challenge the status quo. We design immersive, experiential rather than lecture based workshops.
  • Shift-initiated convenings: For gatherings that we initiate, we arrange for funding through grants and sponsorships, plan and execute the logistics, design the programs, invite you to participate, facilitate the gatherings, and write reports.
  • Collaborative convenings: We invite you to partner with us to stage or sponsor a convening, or we can consult with you to design your own event and program. We can design a convening of field experts around a need or issue your organization wants to address, whether minor tweaks to your programming or institutional reinvention.
  • Analysis and reporting: We share research and information useful to the field. We document, codify, and publish information generated at convenings.

WE CONNECT PEOPLE.

  • We have deep experience and long-standing ties to people in the cultural heritage space and many other interrelated sectors and professional fields.
  • Alliance building: We stage events aimed at connecting people and institutions to create effective, meaningful alliances. We put together teams across institutions, and encourage sharing ideas and building support networks.
  • Networking: We link people to networks, broker/foster relationships between people and institutions, and cultivate relationships with people in the field.
  • Team building: We facilitate internal organizational team building, capacity building, and problem solving.
  • Problem solving: We facilitate convenings of people to address and design solutions to problems in their local communities.

WE HELP YOU BUILD AND MAINTAIN A COMMUNITY-BASED ARCHIVE.

  • We support endeavors to create and share more complete and inclusive documentation of the histories of local communities.
  • Community outreach: We design programming and outreach activities to help communities gather stories, photographs and other materials to build diversified collections for exhibitions, publications, or other uses.
  • Ethical documentation: We develop tools and co-host workshops to help build community practices that support the ethical collection, use, and preservation of social media content as a means of documenting historically significant events.
  • Sustainability modeling: We consult with community leaders to build models for sustainability around archives. We collaborate on grant proposals.

WE HELP YOU IDENTIFY FUNDING AND SUSTAINABILITY MEASURES.

  • We’re social entrepreneurs and use our skills and experience to help other organizations become more sustainable and impactful, especially around engaged cultural heritage.
  • Revenue generation: We help you think more creatively about ways to generate, diversify or grow revenue (streams). For example, we can help you grow your resource equity capacity by exploring ways to restructure your organization to maximize opportunities in real estate investment and property development.
  • Grant writing: We can partner with you to pursue grant funding for collaborative projects. We can also consult with you on your own grant writing projects.
  • Program enrichment: We work with you to design more interactive, exciting and equitable projects that generate revenue.

Community Engagement Tools

We create and customize tools, platforms and programs for community building. We build tools on our own to share with others; and we work with you to create tools and methodologies for your specific community engagement needs.

WE HELP YOU ENGAGE COMMUNITIES YOU WANT TO REACH.

  • We use our expertise and community-centered methodology to help you think differently about community engagement, especially engagement with marginalized groups.
  • Trust building: We design projects to help your institution or organization better connect with people in your local community to develop and build relationships based on trust and equity.
  • Conversation facilitation: We design programs for those in local communities to better connect with one another. We guide organizations to facilitate constructive conversations with community stakeholders in an equitable way. We offer tools, training and support to help create lasting social change in your community.
  • Global connections: We help connect local communities to national and international organizations to facilitate building equitable relationships and lessening extractive practices.
  • Exposure: We develop programs to help national organizations connect with relevant local communities while they are staging their events in those places.

WE BUILD ENGAGEMENT AND DOCUMENTATION TOOLS THAT YOU CAN USE.

  • We create things you can use to engage and document your community, and lead more efficiently.
  • Historypin: Our interactive mapping platform where people share photos and stories, telling the histories of their local communities. cultural organizations expand their communities – and bring their resources to life.
  • One Story Closer: Our community engagement initiative designed to bring together local people to share personal stories and in doing so, create stronger communities built on understanding, empathy and respect.
  • DocNow: Documenting the Now responds to the public's use of social media for chronicling historically significant events as well as demand from scholars, students, and archivists, among others, seeking a user-friendly means of collecting and preserving historically significant digital content. Through specialized tools and workshops, Doc Now builds a community of practice around social media and web archiving.

WE CUSTOMIZE ENGAGEMENT TOOLS FOR YOUR ORGANIZATION.

  • We adapt things we’ve built to suit your particular community engagement needs.
  • Co-designed engagement: You can create projects within the framework of our existing community building tools like Historypin and One Story Closer, or we can work with you to co-design programming using those tools, to meet your specific needs.
  • Customized web tool: We do digital development consulting around Historypin, our online platform for connecting communities with their local history. We can help you organize write-in projects and consult with you to help you think through audience engagement.
  • Customized programming: We can customize One Story Closer, our initiative to help create and amplify more inclusive narratives that reflect a diversity of perspectives. We can work with partners to help gather, collect and share the localized stories of their communities.
