Alexandra Dolan-Mescal

Alexandra is an archivist, front end developer, and user experience designer whose work aims towards increasing capacity for empathy and cultural understanding. She brings these skills together to build a practice of knowledge design, exploring how the design of information informs its consumption and comprehension. She employs community-centered design practices to expand equity and empowerment while building tools for the cultural heritage field. She also advocates for the application of human-centered design principles in the information and archival sciences through graduate teaching, lecture series, and professional engagements.
