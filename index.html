Shift Collective
equity by design

We are a consulting and design collective focused on community-driven design for social change. We create equity-based programs and tools to help organizations better engage and reflect their local communities. We are formerly Shift Design, Inc. Why the new name?

Mission

We challenge norms and push for social change to create a more inclusive and equitable society. We build things to make cultural heritage work more impactful. We respond to the changing needs of a society in crisis with ethics, empathy, and action. We do the work that needs to be done, and connect, support, train, and advocate for others doing the same to push their fields forward.
