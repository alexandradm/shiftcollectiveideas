Shift Collective
equity by design

We are a consulting and design nonprofit focused on community-driven design for social change. We create equity-based programs and tools to help organizations better engage and reflect their local communities. We are formerly Shift Design, Inc. Why the new name?

Our Services

Consulting

Strategic Planning and Design

We design for equity-based organizational change.
Want your organization to be more relevant to your community? We design for institutional change towards greater social equity, primarily in the cultural heritage space.

Convening

Field Building, Field Support

We help advance the cultural heritage field.
We work with cultural heritage practitioners to push the field forward. We offer tools, training and support to help create lasting social change. We guide people and organizations to be better leaders in the field.

Creating

Community Engagement Tools

We help organizations build community.
We build tools on our own to share with others. We create and customize tools to help your organization achieve social, cultural, and resource equity in your community.

How We Work

We're a non-profit organization that designs programs and products to help solve social problems.
Mostly, we work at the intersection of cultural heritage and social justice.
We believe that cultural heritage institutions are well positioned to be at the forefront of fostering social change in [their] local communities. We help organizations both look internally at issues of diversity, inclusion, power and equity, while at the same time find their role in communities at the front lines of social justice, community development, and cultural preservation. We are at a critical juncture in which these organizations have the opportunity to support social change, and empower local communities in both time-tested and new ways.
We help cultural heritage organizations apply community-driven design practices to projects and programs for social change [to reach social equity goals]. We help make your organization more inclusive, representative, and relevant.
Our model for building community as a means for solving social problems works by putting the community at the center of all phases of program and product design. We work within a reproducible, transferable framework for achieving social equity [in narrative, culture and resources]. Using our proven tools and methodology, we bring the results cultural sector leaders need. Together we build the capacity of the heritage sector to effect change.
