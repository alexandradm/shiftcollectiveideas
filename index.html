How We Work

We're a non-profit collective that designs programs and products to help solve social problems, working at the intersection of cultural heritage and social justice.
We believe cultural heritage institutions are well positioned to be at the forefront of fostering social change in local communities. We help organizations both look internally at issues of diversity, inclusion, power and equity, while finding their role in communities at the front lines of social justice, community development, and cultural preservation.

We help cultural heritage organizations apply community-driven design practices to projects and programs. We help make your organization more inclusive, representative, and relevant.

Our model for building community as a means for solving social problems works by putting the community at the center of all phases of program and product design. We work within a reproducible, transferable framework for achieving social equity [in narrative, culture and resources]. Using our proven tools and methodology, we bring the results cultural sector leaders need. Together we build the capacity of the heritage sector to effect change.
